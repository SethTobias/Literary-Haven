Our Story

As a small organization we strive to provide all forms of Literacy across the entire globe. We want to enable every young mind with the same opportunity. To encourage the future of tomorrow to learn and grow. To ensure they are provided with opportunities they never thought they would have.

The Idea That Started It All

The early start of the company all started out as a joke. The idea popped into my head one day when I was assigned a task by my lecturer to create a e-commerce page. At first I had no idea what I wanted my site to revolve around. I thought for a second and blurted out books! My friends at the time laughed and doubted me. They questioned whether or not it suited my character to be selling books. I paid no mind to them my mind was made up and it all started from there. Its funny how those same friends are now the biggest supporters of the company.
